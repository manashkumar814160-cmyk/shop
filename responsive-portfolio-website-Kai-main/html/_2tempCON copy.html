TEMPERATURE AND WEIGHT CONVERTER

Celsius to Fahrenheit Conversion

The formula to convert Celsius to Fahrenheit is: F = (C × 9/5) + 32

Fahrenheit to Celsius Conversion

The formula to convert Fahrenheit to Celsius is: C= (F - 32) × 5/9

Kilograms to Pounds Conversion

The formula to convert Kilograms to Pounds is: Ibs = kg × 2.20462

Pounds to Kilograms Conversion

The formula to convert Pounds to Kilograms is: kg = Ibs ÷ 2.20462
