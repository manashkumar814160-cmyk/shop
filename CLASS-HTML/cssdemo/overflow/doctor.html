Severity Archetypes Explained

🟢 Stable Orbit

Low concern. Patient conditions are controlled, vital signs stable, lab values improving. Preventive maintenance focus.

🟡 Drifting Risk

Early warning. Emerging signs of decline, lab instability, or increasing healthcare utilization. Early intervention recommended.

🔴 Critical Descent

Urgent attention. Rapid deterioration, severe multi-organ involvement, crisis trajectory. Immediate intervention needed.
